Privacy Policy for Orange Upskill Assistant

Last updated: [DATE]

Information We Collect

Our extension processes text that you select on web pages to provide AI-powered assistance. We collect:

  • Selected text content for processing
  • Your AI provider preferences and API keys (stored locally)
  • Feature usage preferences

How We Use Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide AI-powered text analysis and assistance
  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Improve the extension's functionality

Data Storage and Security

Your API keys and preferences are stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage API. Selected text is sent to your chosen AI provider (OpenAI, Google) for processing according to their privacy policies.

Third-Party Services

This extension integrates with:

  • OpenAI API - subject to OpenAI's privacy policy
  • Google Gemini API - subject to Google's privacy policy

Data Retention

We do not store your text selections. API keys and preferences remain on your device until you uninstall the extension.
