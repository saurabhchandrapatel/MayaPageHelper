Privacy Policy for Maya Page Helper

Last updated: December 2024

Open Source & No Tracking

Maya Page Helper is an open-source extension that does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. This extension operates entirely on your device.

Data Processing

The extension processes:

  • Text you select on web pages (processed locally and sent only to your chosen AI provider)
  • Your AI provider preferences (stored locally on your device)
  • Your API keys (stored locally on your device only)

No Data Collection

We do not:

  • Collect or store your personal information
  • Track your browsing activity
  • Send data to our servers (we don't have any)
  • Use analytics or tracking services

Third-Party AI Services

When you use AI features, selected text is sent directly to your chosen provider:

  • OpenAI (if you choose to use their API)
  • Google Gemini (if you choose to use their API)
  • Local AI models (if you configure local endpoints)

These services have their own privacy policies that govern how they handle your data.

Local Storage Only

All settings and API keys are stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly sync it through Chrome's sync feature.

Open Source

This extension is open source. You can review the code to verify our privacy practices.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, please create an issue in the project's GitHub repository.
